Our Story: East Africa's Baker's Heart

Baking has a rich and fascinating history, dating back to the Stone Age when people used hot stones or embers to cook rudimentary flatbreads. The Egyptians, Greeks, and Romans further developed baking techniques, introducing ovens and incorporating sweeteners like honey and fruits.

During the Middle Ages, specialized bakers emerged in Europe, experimenting with flours, grains, and leavening agents. The introduction of sugar from the Arab world expanded the possibilities for sweet baked goods.

The Industrial Revolution revolutionized baking with more efficient ovens and commercially produced flour, making baked goods more accessible to all.

Today, baking is a global art form, with bakers constantly innovating and creating unique treats. At Octofarm, we carry on this tradition, crafting delicious baked goods with passion and quality ingredients.
